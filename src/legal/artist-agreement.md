# FlyMusic Artist Agreement

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

Welcome to FlyMusic as an artist. This agreement covers your rights and responsibilities when sharing your music on our platform.

---

## 1. Your Music, Your Rights

**You own your music.** FlyMusic does not claim ownership of any content you upload.

When you upload music to FlyMusic, you grant us a non-exclusive, worldwide license to:

- Display and stream your content on the platform
- Promote your content within FlyMusic
- Create thumbnails and previews
- Enable fan engagement features (likes, shares, playlists)

This license ends when you remove your content from FlyMusic.

---

## 2. Your Responsibilities

### You confirm that:

- You own or have rights to all content you upload
- Your content doesn't infringe on others' copyrights
- You have necessary permissions for samples, features, or collaborations
- Your content complies with our content guidelines

### You agree to:

- Provide accurate metadata (titles, credits, descriptions)
- Not upload content that violates others' rights
- Take responsibility for any copyright claims on your content

---

## 3. Upload Guidelines

When uploading to FlyMusic:

- **Audio formats:** MP3, WAV, FLAC accepted
- **Quality:** Upload the best quality available
- **Metadata:** Complete and accurate information helps fans find you
- **Cover art:** You must have rights to any images used

---

## 4. Earnings & Payments

### Supporter Revenue

If you enable Supporter Pass:

- **Stripe Connect required.** You must connect a Stripe account to receive payments.
- **Revenue split:** You receive 70% of supporter payments. FlyMusic retains 30% (includes payment processing fees).
- **Payouts:** Processed through Stripe according to their schedule.

### No Guarantees

FlyMusic does not guarantee:

- Any specific income or earnings
- Number of plays, followers, or supporters
- Success in Spotlight campaigns
- Placement in recommendations or discovery

Your success depends on many factors including your music, engagement, and audience building.

---

## 5. Spotlight Campaigns

When participating in Spotlight:

- Voting is community-driven
- Results depend on fan engagement
- Historical performance doesn't guarantee future results
- We ensure fair voting practices

---

## 6. Content Removal

You can remove your content at any time. When you do:

- It will be removed from public view
- Existing playlists may show the content as unavailable
- Historical analytics may be retained
- Cached versions may persist temporarily

---

## 7. Account Termination

We may suspend or terminate artist accounts that:

- Violate these terms or the User Agreement
- Upload infringing content repeatedly
- Engage in fraudulent activity
- Manipulate platform features

---

## 8. Changes to Terms

We may update this agreement. Significant changes will be communicated, and continued use constitutes acceptance.
