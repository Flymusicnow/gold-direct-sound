# FlyMusic Brand Portal Terms

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

These terms govern brand partnerships and promotional activities on FlyMusic.

---

## 1. Brand Access

### Eligibility

Brand Portal access is:

- Granted by FlyMusic at our discretion
- Subject to approval and ongoing compliance
- Revocable if terms are violated

### Account Requirements

Brands must:

- Provide accurate business information
- Designate authorized representatives
- Maintain appropriate business credentials
- Comply with applicable advertising laws

---

## 2. Campaign Guidelines

### Sponsored Content

All sponsored content must:

- Be clearly identified as sponsored or promotional
- Comply with FTC guidelines and local advertising laws
- Not mislead users about the nature of content
- Include appropriate disclosures

### Content Standards

Brand content must:

- Align with FlyMusic community values
- Not exploit or misrepresent artists
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Follow platform content guidelines

---

## 3. Artist Relationships

### Ethical Standards

Brands must:

- Respect artist autonomy and creative control
- Not pressure artists into unfavorable terms
- Honor agreed compensation and timelines
- Maintain transparent communication

### Prohibited Practices

Brands may not:

- Exploit emerging artists
- Make false promises about exposure or compensation
- Require artists to compromise their artistic integrity
- Engage in exclusivity demands without fair compensation

---

## 4. Data & Privacy

### Data Access

- Brand access to platform data is limited and defined
- User data cannot be exported or used outside FlyMusic
- Analytics provided are aggregate, not individual
- Compliance with GDPR and privacy laws is required

### Data Use Restrictions

Brands may not:

- Collect user personal data through campaigns
- Use FlyMusic data for external marketing
- Share access credentials or data with third parties
- Attempt to identify individual users from aggregate data

## 5. Payments & Fees

### Campaign Costs

- Fees are outlined in your brand agreement
- Payment terms as specified in your contract
- Refunds subject to campaign performance guarantees
- Late payments may result in campaign suspension
