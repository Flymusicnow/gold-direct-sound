# FlyMusic Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

---

## 1. Who We Are

FlyMusic is a music platform connecting artists directly with fans. This policy applies to all FlyMusic services.

**Data Controller:** FlyMusic AB  
**Contact:** privacy@flymusic.se

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Account Information

- Email address
- Display name
- Profile information you provide
- Authentication data

### Usage Data

- Listening history and preferences
- Interactions (likes, follows, comments)
- Spotlight votes
- Device and browser information
- IP address

### Payment Data

- Payment transactions (processed by Stripe)
- Subscription history
- We do not store full payment card details

### Content Data

- Music and videos you upload (artists)
- Playlists you create
- Comments and messages

---

## 3. Why We Collect Data

### Lawful Bases (GDPR)

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Account creation & management | Contract |
| Processing payments | Contract |
| Personalized recommendations | Legitimate interest |
| Platform analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Marketing communications | Consent |
| Legal compliance | Legal obligation |

### Specific Uses

- **To provide our service:** Account management, content delivery, payments
- **To improve the platform:** Analytics, feature development, bug fixes
- **To personalize your experience:** Recommendations, taste profiles
- **To communicate:** Service updates, notifications you've enabled
- **To ensure safety:** Fraud prevention, security, content moderation

---

## 4. How Long We Keep Data

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Usage analytics | 2 years |
| Marketing consent | Until withdrawn |
| Security logs | 1 year |

---

## 5. Who We Share Data With

### Service Providers

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Analytics tools:** Platform improvement

### Legal Requirements

We may share data when required by law or to:

- Comply with legal process
- Protect rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse

### We Never Sell Your Data

Your personal data is never sold to third parties.

---

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your data.

### Rectification
Correct inaccurate data.

### Erasure
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").

### Portability
Receive your data in a portable format.

### Objection
Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

### Restriction
Request limited processing in certain cases.

### Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for consent-based processing at any time.

### How to Exercise Rights

Contact privacy@flymusic.se or use Settings in your account.

We respond to requests within 30 days.

---

## 7. Cookies & Tracking

### Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function:

- Authentication
- Session management
- Security

### Analytics Cookies

Help us understand platform usage:

- Page views
- Feature engagement
- Performance metrics

### Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect functionality.

---

## 8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the EEA. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

- Standard contractual clauses
- Data processing agreements
- Privacy Shield compliance (where applicable)

---

## 9. Security

We protect your data through:

- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Incident response procedures

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

FlyMusic is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. When we do:

- We'll update the "Last Updated" date
- Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification
- Continued use after changes means acceptance
