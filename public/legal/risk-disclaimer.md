# FlyMusic Risk Disclaimer

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

This disclaimer provides important information about risks and expectations when using FlyMusic.

---

## 1. No Income Guarantee

### For Artists

FlyMusic provides tools to share music and connect with fans. However:

- **No guaranteed income.** We cannot promise any specific earnings.
- **Variable support.** Fan support fluctuates based on many factors.
- **Market conditions.** Music industry economics affect all platforms.
- **Platform changes.** Features and revenue models may evolve.

### Earnings Depend On

- Quality and appeal of your music
- Your engagement with fans
- Marketing efforts outside FlyMusic
- Broader market conditions
- Competition and discovery dynamics

---

## 2. XP & Spotlight

### XP Is Not Currency

- XP reflects engagement, not monetary value
- XP cannot be exchanged for money
- XP balances may change due to system updates
- XP provides recognition, not financial reward

### Spotlight Performance

- Past results don't predict future outcomes
- Voting is influenced by community preference
- High rankings don't guarantee career success
- Campaign results vary widely

---

## 3. Supporter Transactions

### For Fans

When supporting artists:

- **Payments are final.** Supporter fees are non-refundable.
- **Value is subjective.** Exclusive content varies by artist.
- **Subscriptions recur.** Cancel before renewal to stop charges.
- **Benefits may change.** Artists control their supporter offerings.

### For Artists

When receiving support:

- **Income varies.** Don't depend solely on FlyMusic income.
- **Fans may cancel.** Subscriber counts fluctuate.
- **Platform fees apply.** FlyMusic retains 30% of supporter payments.
- **Payment processing.** Stripe handles payouts with their terms.

- **Uptime not guaranteed.** Technical issues may occur.

- **Data risks.** Despite our best efforts, data loss can occur.
