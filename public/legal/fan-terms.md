# FlyMusic Fan Terms

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

Welcome to the FlyMusic community. These terms cover your experience as a fan on our platform.

---

## 1. Supporting Artists

### Supporter Pass

When you become a supporter:

- **Transactions are final.** Supporter payments are non-refundable.
- **Subscription billing.** Charges recur according to your selected plan.
- **Cancellation.** You can cancel anytime, but no refunds for the current period.
- **Artist benefits.** 70% of your support goes directly to the artist.

### What You Get

As a supporter, you may receive:

- Exclusive content from artists
- Early access to releases
- Special badges and recognition
- Direct connection with artists

Benefits vary by artist and tier.

---

## 2. XP & Achievements

### Understanding XP

- XP reflects your engagement on FlyMusic
- XP is **not currency** and has no monetary value
- XP cannot be exchanged, sold, or transferred
- XP balances may be adjusted for system integrity

### Achievements

- Unlock achievements through genuine engagement
- Achievements are personal recognition, not rewards
- We may add, modify, or retire achievements

---

## 3. Spotlight Voting

### Voting Integrity

When voting in Spotlight campaigns:

- Vote authentically for music you enjoy
- Don't attempt to manipulate votes
- One vote per entry per campaign (unless you use boost tokens)
- Report suspicious voting activity

### Fair Play

We monitor for:

- Automated voting
- Vote manipulation schemes
- Coordinated artificial voting
- Multiple account voting

Violations may result in vote removal or account action.

---

## 4. Community Standards

### Be Respectful

- Treat artists and fellow fans with respect
- Engage constructively in comments
- Don't harass, spam, or bully anyone
- Support the community you want to be part of

### Content Interaction

When commenting or engaging:

- Keep it relevant and respectful
- No hate speech or discrimination
- No explicit or inappropriate content
- Report violations to help us maintain standards

## 5. Playlists & Stacks

### Your Collections

- Playlists you create are yours to manage
- Public playlists can be discovered by others
- We may feature exceptional playlists
- Content availability depends on artist decisions
